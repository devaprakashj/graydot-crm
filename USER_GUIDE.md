# 🛡️ GrayDot OS - Universal User Guide

Welcome to the **GrayDot OS** Lead & Project Management CRM. This guide explains the 10-step workflow across all roles to ensure maximum efficiency.

---

## 👥 Roles & Responsibilities

### 1. 🏗️ Lead Generation (Role: "Lead")
**Your Goal:** Generate high-quality leads from various sources (Google, Instagram, Referrals).
*   **Action:** Go to the "Leads" tab and click "Add New Lead".
*   **Workflow:** Once created, a Sales member is automatically assigned to the lead via round-robin.
*   **Tracking:** Monitor your daily quota progress on the Overview dashboard.

### 2. 📞 Sales Team (Role: "Sales")
**Your Goal:** Contact the client, understand their needs, and determine if they are "Interested".
*   **Action:** Update the lead status to "Contacted", then "Interested" if the deal moves forward.
*   **Requirement:** Ensure the "Requirement" field is detailed before saving as Interested.
*   **Impact:** Interested leads move to the Manager's approval queue.

### 3. 🎩 Manager (Role: "Manager")
**Your Goal:** Filter junk leads, allocate resources, and set project timelines.
*   **Action:** Approve "Interested" leads by assigning a **Price**, **Developer**, and **Deadline**.
*   **Impact:** This converts a lead into an active **Project** for the Developer.

### 4. 💻 Developer (Role: "Developer")
**Your Goal:** Execute the project according to the client's requirements.
*   **Action:** Change task status from "In Progress" to "Completed" once the work is done.
*   **Impact:** Completed work automatically notifies the QA Tester.

### 5. 🔍 QA Tester (Role: "Tester")
**Your Goal:** Ensure 100% quality before client delivery.
*   **Action:** Review the developer's work. Mark as **"Pass"** (moves to final approval) or **"Fail"** (returns to Dev with feedback).
*   **Impact:** High quality ensures repeat clients and professional reputation.

### 6. 👑 Admin (Role: "Admin")
**Your Goal:** System maintenance and high-level monitoring.
*   **Team Management:** Switch members' status between 'Active' and 'Disabled'.
*   **Data Health:** Use the **"Repair & Format Sheets"** button in the Pipeline tab if headers are missing or formatting looks broken.
*   **Reporting:** Download the entire pipeline as a professional **PDF Report**.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 🛠️ Repair & Format Sheets
If your Google Sheet looks unorganized or is missing headers:
1.  Go to **Admin Dashboard**.
2.  Click **Pipeline** tab.
3.  Click the orange **"Repair & Format Sheets"** button.
4.  The system will automatically restore Midnight Blue headers and auto-resize all columns.

### 📄 PDF Report Generation
Found in both Admin and Manager views under the Pipeline tab. Generates a branded document for stakeholder review.

### 🤖 AI Assistant (Antigravity)
Click the floating star icon for help with navigation or summarizing lead statuses!
